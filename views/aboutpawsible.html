About It's Pawsible

It's Pawsible is a home grown business created and ran by Carol Ann Bambey, who resides in The Finger Lakes in New York State.
Born an animal lover, Carol spends her days rescuing animals and painting them in her down time.
Carol has been an artist her whole life and finds great satisfaction in bringing joy to her customers through her art.
She hand-paints a variety of glassware to her cutomers desire.
Carol paints a range of subjects like cats, dogs, barnyard and other animals, along with plants, flowers, and scenery.
She takes custom orders, takes pride in her work, and takes great care of her clients.
