Services

The Process:

  • Find and send in a picture of what you want painted.
  • Pick out what kind of glassware you want painted.
  • Collaberate and discuss the details with Carol.
  • Carol works her Magic.
  • You recieve a one-of-a-kind piece of art.
  • Due to high demand, you can only place orders by contacting Carol.

Pricing:

  • Base rate includes one item of glassware and one subject.
  • For additional subjects add $7 each.
  • Custom fee is $5 for special orders.
  • Prices start at $15 and increase with the size of the glassware you want painted.
  • All prices are subject to taxes and shipping.

You can be sure that with each piece, you will recieve a unique hand painted piece of art with outstanding quality.
